Gell Product

Gel nail polish is a long-lasting and durable type of polish that provides a high-gloss finish. It requires curing under a UV or LED lamp to harden and set the polish, resulting in a chip-resistant manicure that can last up to two weeks. Gel polishes come in a wide range of colors and finishes, and they are known for their shiny appearance. They require specific removal techniques using acetone or gel polish remover
